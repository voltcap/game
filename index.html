Unfolding the Kapulukaya Hoard

Consular Solidus Valentinian II (372-392 AD)

Historical Context

This gold solidus was issued during the reign of Valentinian II, who became Western Roman Emperor as a child and ruled under the influence of senior co-emperors and court officials. The coin reflects the political realities of the late fourth century, when imperial legitimacy was reinforced through iconography, titulature, and dynastic representation.
